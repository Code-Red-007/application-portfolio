What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions.

How We Use Cookies

  • To improve your browsing experience on our site.
  • To remember your preferences (such as your cookie consent).
  • To monitor and analyze site performance (anonymous statistics only).
