LBBD DRIVE VALUES

Deliver, Respond, Inspire, Value and Engage

Here is a list of LBBD values.

VALUE DETAILS
DELIVER
I get the basics right and keep learning my skills.

I work with my teams and others to get things done, and never leave a problem unsolved

I am responsible for my actions, make decisions and can explain why I make them
RESPOND
I am professional and polite in every contact I have with residents and customers,

leaving them always with the good impression of the council
INSPIRE
I see it as my personal responsiblity to maximise the satisfactions of customers;

treating customers fairly, professionally and transparently



I take pride in my work , and represent the council in a positive way

sending the right message to residents, customers and partners in a way

I communicate and behave
VALUE I understand my contribution and the part I play to change the borough for the better.
ENGAGE I speak up constructively and with respect when I think I need things to improve.
